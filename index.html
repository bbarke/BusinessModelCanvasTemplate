The Business Model Canvas - RSA Encryption

Key Partners

Our key partners would be the community that develops extensions for Google Chrome.

The key resources we obtain from that community would be the code, examples, and knowledge to create theis extentsion

Key Activities

Distribution channels - Google Chrome extensions

It would need to be distributed in a way others can easily obtain it.

It would solve the problem of people reading your email messages

Value Proposition

The value we deliver to the customer is safety to their messages to make sure only the intended viewers can view the page

Customer Relationship

Each customer must realize the importance of keeping their information safe.

There are free RSA libraries for encryption, so that will keep the cost down

The major cost here is time

Customer Segments

We are creating value for those that want to keep their information safe.

To prevent others from viewing their sensitive information

Key Resources

A huge key resource here would be Google Chrome. It is the program we are creating the extension for

Another key resource is time. We need to put in the time to create this extension

Channels

We can reach our customers though the internet, on websites that promote encryption.

The most cost effective channel is though free postings on RSA websites.

Currently, people are unaware that their information is being stored and viewed by others.

The channel that works the best is one where people are already seeking security, but we would need to reach to other channels as well to help those that do not know about their information being stored.

Cost Structure

The most important cost structure is time.

Since this is a class project, we are essentially paying for this extension to be built, but maybe people on the web will donate to our cause?

Revenue Streams

I believe people would pay a little money for a great encryption extension. However, most of the chrome extensions are free.

To create revenue, however, we could place a donate box on the page they retrieve the extension from
